The methodology

How the True Line actually works.

Most odds screens treat one sportsbook as gospel and call that the True Line. We don't. Each market is unique, every book has different biases by sport and prop type, and lines move for reasons. Our True Line is weighted per market, backtested continuously, and built off the most reliable reference books in the industry.

01

Historical book efficiency

We weight each book differently by sport, market, and prop type based on how sharp their pricing has been historically. Pinnacle's NBA spread isn't the same signal as their NFL alt totals — and we price them accordingly.

02

Line move direction & timing

When and how a market moves matters as much as where it lands. Sharp early action, steam moves, and reverse line movement are all factored into how confidently we price each number.

03

Bias correction by prop type

Player props, alts, and exotic markets carry structural biases the sides-and-totals world doesn't. We adjust per prop type so a +EV alt isn't masked by a market that's systemically off.

04

Offshore + sharp-out reference set

More offshore and sharp-out books than any other provider — the ones that actually move first when smart money lands. The True Line is built off where the market is going, not where the public books are stuck.

The result: a True Line you can backtest, trust, and scale off — priced across every book, every market, every second.
