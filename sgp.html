The Problem

Why in-house SGP pricing leaks margin.

SGPs are the highest-margin product in your book — and the most error-prone. Correlation modeling at scale is hard. Your quants can't cover every market. Sharps target the legs where you're off. The result is a product that's making you money on aggregate but bleeding it on every market a competitor prices sharper.

Correlation modeling is hard at scale

Pricing two correlated legs is straightforward. Pricing five legs across markets with shifting correlations as the line moves is a different problem entirely — and the one most in-house systems get wrong.

Sharps target your softest legs

Sophisticated bettors don't beat your SGP product on the headline price — they beat it on the leg where your model is off. One mispriced player prop multiplied across a parlay is where margin walks out the door.

Coverage gaps cost you volume

Markets you can't price you can't offer. Players you can't model, sports you don't cover, props your quants haven't built — every gap is volume your customers take to the book that prices it.

In-house systems lag the market

Your model trained on last season's data, refreshed quarterly, can't keep up with a line that moved twice in the last hour. Sharps trade the lag — and SGPs are where the lag compounds across legs.

The methodology

How the engine actually prices.

Every leg in your customer's parlay gets priced against the Betstamp True Line — the same model that runs the odds screen for the sharp bettors moving these markets in the first place. Then we layer correlation modeling on top, calibrated continuously against closing-line outcomes.

01

True Line per-leg pricing

Every leg priced independently against our market-weighted True Line — backtested on 5+ seasons of closing lines, weighted per-market by the historical sharpness of every reference book.

02

Multi-leg correlation matrices

Correlation between team total / spread / player props / alt totals is modeled jointly, not bolted on. Matrices update continuously as the underlying lines move, so the parlay price reflects the live game state.

03

Continuous backtesting

Every priced SGP is benchmarked against actual outcomes and against every book offering the same parlay. The engine recalibrates daily — no quarterly model freeze.

04

Reference set across 200+ books

More offshore and sharp-out reference books than any other provider — the books that actually move first when smart money lands. Our pricing reflects where the market is going, not where the public books have settled.

The result: pricing your customers can't beat — across every market your in-house quants can't fully model.
