The Company

The pricing intelligence layer for sports betting.

Pricing intelligence, market data, and historical odds for sportsbooks, operators, and professional bettors.

What we do

Providing the
infrastructure
behind sharp
prop markets.

Betstamp builds pricing intelligence and market data for sportsbooks and professional bettors. We turn 200+ books, exchanges, and prediction markets into one sharp, real-time view of fair value.

That work spans real-time and historical odds, fair-value pricing on player props and alternates, correlation-aware same-game-parlay pricing, and a single normalized data feed across the market — delivered by API or through our own products. Operators price sharper and close coverage gaps with it; bettors use it to find edge the market hasn't corrected yet.

Betstamp launched in 2020, built by professional bettors who needed sharper tools than the market offered — and won at scale using them. That same pricing now runs as infrastructure for sportsbooks, operators, and the sharpest bettors in the game.

Because it's still used to win real money, the pricing can't drift. Every model is backtested against years of closing lines, monitored continuously, and sharpened over time — what operators run on is the pricing we stake our own edge on.
